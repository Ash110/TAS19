Mini Certification

menu

Don't want to spend all day in a classroom? Get a chance to be certified in one of 3 offered certifications; entrepreneurship, photography and cybersecurity. Don't worry, we won't take up too much of your time, you will be out in time for your next event.

  1. Photography :
    With Instagram, Snapchat and Ticktoc, being the front runners of social media, getting the right camera angle has never been more important. In our Into to Photography, we will ensure you leave equipped with the skills to never have a bad post ever again. And here is the best part, you don’t need a fancy camera, all you need is your phone!
  2. Entrepreneurship :
    Have a great start up idea? But no clue as to how to start? How do you start a company, when all you have is an idea? Now that’s where we come in. We can teach you how the mind of an entrepreneur works, teach you how to make something amazing, out of nothing.
  3. Cyber security:
    The best way to outsmart someone, is to become them. Learn how they think, what makes them tick. So that’s how we approach cyber security, not by hiding and avoiding, but by penetrating and attacking our own system to see where it fails. Fix it, so that the real hackers can’t get in.

General Rules :

  1. Each certification will be for a duration of around 1 hour.
  2. The certification will start on based on class size.
  3. Classes will run throughout the duration of the fest, and will only start on demand.
  4. If you are interested, you may submit in assignments after the class.
