Sure, making an ordinary robot is lot of fun. You can control it with a remote or even with a few buttons. But haven’t you always wanted your robot to control itself? To be able to observe its surroundings and then move according to what’s around it? Imagine making a robot that can sense the intensity of light around it and then move accordingly. Well, we are going to make that a reality at the TAS Workshop. We are shedding new light on robotics, teaching you how to make robots that are controlled by their surroundings. Take your electronics skills to the next level, giving your creations intelligence, letting them find the light.

Join us on the 24th and be apart of the new age!

Rules :
  1. Teams of upto 4.
  2. No prerequisites; all materials will be supplied to you.
  3. The morning session will be from 9-12, and the afternoon session from 12:30-3:30
