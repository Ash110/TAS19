The Inquisitors Trial

Debate

menu `

You have one speech to prove that you are undoubtedly right, construct the perfect argument and leave your opponent in shambles. Take part in The Amateur Scientist's debate contest to put your skills to the test. Let's see if you have what it takes to be the last debater standing!


Preliminaries:
  1. Teams of 2; opening and closing speaker.
  2. Each speaker has 2 mins to speak, giving the team a total time period of 4 mins; this includes rebuttals.
  3. Teams will have 8 mins of preparation time once they receive their topic.
  4. Teams are not allowed to use the internet during prep time.
  5. Teams may have a reference sheet while speaking.

Finals:

The 8 best teams will compete against each other in 5 separate rounds.
