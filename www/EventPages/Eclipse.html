Eclipse

Shadow Art

menu

Shadows, the obstruction of light, the beauty and mystery they hold. Turn these shadows into art, making everyday objects seem extraordinary. Showcase your artistic ability and test the bounds of your creativity during eclipse/ silhouette.


Rules :
  1. Upto 3 members per team.
  2. Each team will be provided with one A1 sized board which they will be required to paint/model and cut out according to the theme given on spot.
  3. The final design must convey an idea in light and its shadow must convey a deeper meaning of the given theme.
  4. The students will be judged on the basis of the creativity and relevance of the design with respect to the theme.
  5. Time Limit: 2 hrs
