Lost and abandoned. Nothing but a map and the clothes on your back to find your way through. Will you be able to solve the puzzles and make it to the end of your journey?


Rules :
    For students from class 8 to class 12/PUC only. Maximum of 2 students in a team.
  1. The team must consist of participants from the same school/college.
  2. Cross-school/cross-college teams are NOT allowed.
  3. ID card is mandatory.
  4. No limit to number of teams participating from a particular school/college.
  5. Top 8 teams qualify into the live final round.
  6. Only the best team from a particular school/college will get to participate in the finals.
  7. Answer sheets and other required stationery will be provided on spot.
  8. NO form of electronic gadgets/cheat sheets allowed into the quiz, for any reasons. Teams resorting to unfair practices, if any, will be instantly disqualified without notice.
  9. Quizmaster's decision is final and binding.
