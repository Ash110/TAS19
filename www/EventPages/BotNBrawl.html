Preliminaries: Maze Runner

The participants will have to navigate their bots through a confusing maze under gruelling pressure and collect a key present within the maze which unlocks a door to make their exit from the maze

  1. No. of participants in a team: Upto 3
  2. Time limit- 3 min
  3. Each team gets only ONE chance.
  4. Once the time limit gets over, the participant must leave the maze.
  5. The score will be decided based on how quickly the bot completes the maze.
  6. Top 32 teams will qualify for the finals.

Finals: Capture the Flag

The participant will face off against 3 other challengers in an obstacle filled arena. The objective of the player is to find and bring back their colour flag back to their own base in the shortest amount of time.

  1. There can be only 4 challengers in the arena at a time.
  2. The participant get only one chance.
  3. The participant can push, block the path of other challengers.
  4. The maximum time limit for each match is 6 minutes.
  5. In the quarter-finals and semi-finals, each player has to get their own flag back to their base.
  6. The finals will consist of a common flag amongst 4 players. The player that brings the flag back to their
    7. base first wins.
Bot Specifications

  1. Length: 130-170 mm
  2. Width: 130-170 mm
  3. Distance of base from ground: 200-400 mm
  4. Weight: 300-700 g
Note : Teams not abiding by the specifications will be eliminated by default.
